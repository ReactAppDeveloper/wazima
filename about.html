Simplifying Healthcare,
Connecting Lives

A platform that brings healthcare to your fingertips, making care accessible for patients and manageable for doctors.

Our mission is to provide a secure, convenient platform that enables patients to receive the care they need, while helping doctors efficiently manage their practice. By leveraging technology, we aim to remove the barriers that traditionally separate patients and healthcare providers.

Our core values

Fueled by a strong belief in accessibility, collaboration, customization, and modern technology, Wazima is rewriting the future of healthcare.
  • Responsibility
    Beautiful software should be accessible to every health practitioner around the world. This is why removing geographical, financial, and technological barriers to great software is at the forefront of Wazima Health™’s mission.
  • Collaboration
    People do their best work when they do it together. Wazima Health™ believes deeply in the strength of collaboration and that by combining ideas and knowledge, we can create a more effective, efficient, and safe healthcare system.
  • Impact & Innovation
    At Wazima Health™, we believe in working the way you want. Healthcare practitioners deserve to work according to their own needs – not someone else’s. That’s why we celebrate and encourage ownership, personality, and customization.
  • Health
    We believe in the power of modern technology. Combined with the right ideas, knowledge, and execution, technology can change the future of healthcare for both patients and practitioners. And that’s exactly what we’re aiming to do.
